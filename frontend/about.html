Our Story

Crafting Change, One Purchase at a Time

We're building a community where talented Washington artists can thrive while every sale contributes to solving homelessness in our state.

Our Mission

To create economic opportunity for Washington's artists—including those experiencing homelessness—while funding solutions to our state's homelessness crisis through every transaction on our platform.

How It Works

01

Artists Join

Local Washington artists create free accounts and list their handmade products on our marketplace.

02

You Shop

Browse unique, handcrafted items and purchase directly from talented creators.

03

Impact Made

5% of every sale goes directly to organizations fighting homelessness in Washington.
