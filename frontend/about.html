Our Story

Crafting Change, One Purchase at a Time

We're building a community where talented creative voices are brought forth to express themselves by creating unique pieces, and every purchase helps our neighbors by supporting solutions to homelessness in our communities.

Our Mission

To create economic opportunity for artists across the United States—including those experiencing homelessness—while ensuring that every purchase on our platform helps fund real solutions to our nation's homelessness crisis.

How It Works

01

Artists Join

Local Washington artists create free accounts and list their handmade products on our marketplace.

02

You Shop

Browse unique, handcrafted items and purchase directly from talented creators.

03

Impact Made

5% of every sale goes directly to organizations fighting homelessness in Washington.

What We Believe

Creativity Heals

Art provides not just income, but purpose, dignity, and a path forward for those facing challenges.

Connection Matters

When you buy handmade, you're not just getting a product—you're supporting a real person's story.

Small Actions Add Up

5% from each sale might seem small, but together we're building real change in our communities.

Transparency First

We publish quarterly impact reports so you can see exactly where your contribution goes.
